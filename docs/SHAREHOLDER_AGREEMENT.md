## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to set forth their respective rights, obligations, and responsibilities with respect to ownership of the Company and the distribution of net profits;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Net Profit"** means the Company's total revenue less all operating expenses, taxes, cost of goods sold, interest payments, depreciation, amortization, and any other deductions as determined by the Company's certified accountant or auditor in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP).

1.2 **"Profit Distribution Period"** means each calendar quarter (or fiscal year, as agreed), at the end of which Net Profits shall be calculated and distributed.

1.3 **"Shares"** means the equity interest held by each Shareholder as described in this Agreement.

---

## 2. SHAREHOLDING STRUCTURE

2.1 The Minority Shareholder holds a **15% (fifteen percent)** equity stake in the Company.

2.2 This 15% stake entitles the Minority Shareholder to receive **15% of all Net Profits** distributed by the Company.

2.3 The shareholding percentages shall not be altered without written consent of all Parties.

---

## 3. PROFIT DISTRIBUTION

3.1 **Entitlement.** The Minority Shareholder shall be entitled to receive **15% of the Company's Net Profit** for each Profit Distribution Period.

3.2 **Calculation.** Net Profit shall be calculated after:
   - All operational costs and expenses
   - Employee salaries and contractor payments
   - Taxes and governmental levies
   - Loan repayments and interest
   - Reinvestment reserves as agreed by the majority shareholders (not to exceed ____% of gross revenue without consent of Minority Shareholder)

3.3 **Payment Schedule.** Profit distributions shall be made:
   - [x] **Monthly** — on or before the **5th day** of each following month
   - [ ] Quarterly — within 30 days after the end of each calendar quarter
   - [ ] Annually — within 60 days after the end of each fiscal year

3.4 **Minimum Distribution Threshold.** No distribution shall be required if the Net Profit for any period is less than $100.00 USD. Undistributed amounts shall carry forward to the next period.

3.5 **Payment Method.** All distributions shall be paid via bank transfer to the Minority Shareholder's nominated account:
   - Bank: _____________________________
   - Account Name: _____________________________
   - Account Number: _____________________________
   - IBAN/SWIFT: _____________________________

3.6 **Audit Rights.** The Minority Shareholder shall have the right, upon 14 days' written notice, to request an independent audit of the Company's financial records to verify Net Profit calculations. The cost of such audit shall be borne by the requesting party unless material discrepancies (>5%) are found, in which case the Company shall bear the cost.

---

## 4. FINANCIAL REPORTING

4.1 The Company shall provide the Minority Shareholder with:
   - Monthly revenue and expense summaries (within 15 days of month end)
   - Quarterly profit and loss statements (within 30 days of quarter end)
   - Annual audited financial statements (within 90 days of fiscal year end)

4.2 All financial reports shall be prepared by a qualified accountant and certified as accurate.

---

## 5. REINVESTMENT & RESERVES

5.1 The Parties may unanimously agree to reinvest a portion of Net Profits back into the Company for growth purposes.

5.2 Any reinvestment decision that reduces distributable profit must be approved in writing by the Minority Shareholder if the reinvestment exceeds ____% of Net Profit in any given period.

---

## 6. TRANSFER OF SHARES

6.1 **Right of First Refusal.** Neither Party may sell, transfer, or assign their shares without first offering those shares to the other Party at the same price and terms.

6.2 **Lock-Up Period.** Neither Party may transfer shares for a period of _____ months/years from the date of this Agreement without written consent of the other Party.

6.3 **Change of Control.** In the event of a sale, merger, or acquisition of the Company, the Minority Shareholder shall receive 15% of the net proceeds after deduction of transaction costs and outstanding liabilities.

---

## 7. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

7.1 The Minority Shareholder:
   - Holds 15% equity and profit rights
   - Has no obligation to participate in daily operations unless separately agreed
   - Shall not be held personally liable for Company debts beyond their investment

7.2 The Majority Shareholder(s):
   - Manage day-to-day operations
   - Make operational decisions without requiring Minority Shareholder approval, except as noted herein
   - Are responsible for accurate financial reporting

---

## 8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 The Parties shall first attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation within 30 days of written notice.

8.2 If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to mediation before a mutually agreed mediator.

8.3 If mediation fails, disputes shall be finally resolved by binding arbitration in _________________ (jurisdiction) under the rules of _________________.
