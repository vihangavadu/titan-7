Common Questions

How do I get Titan OS?

Contact Lucid Empire to discuss licensing. After purchase, you receive the full V9.1 ISO image, deployment scripts, and documentation.

What VPS do I need?

Any Debian 12 KVM VPS with 4+ CPU cores and 4GB+ RAM. Recommended: 8 cores, 8GB+ RAM, 100GB+ disk. Tested on Hostinger KVM 8.

Is custom development available?

Yes. Professional and Enterprise licenses include custom module development, AI model fine-tuning, and feature requests directly from Lucid.

What support is included?

All licenses include email support. Professional tier adds priority support. Enterprise tier includes dedicated support from Lucid with SLA guarantee.
